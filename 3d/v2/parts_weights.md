# Weight Measurement Methodology

Randomly pick 10 pieces of an item from a bag and measure with a calibrated precision scale. Divide the measured weight by 10. Repeat the procedure with the remaining parts 5â€“15 times depending on the total quantity, then remove the minimum and maximum values and take the median of the rest. Use 3 significant figures.

__Weight scale__: Vibra DJ-150 (Minimum unit: 0.01g, calibrated with calibration weights)
